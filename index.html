our vision

Our vision for the VinFuture Prize is to catalyze meaningful change in everyday lives, i.e., tangible and highly scalable improvements in areas such as productivity, prosperity, connectivity, health, safety, environment, sustainability, as well as the overall happiness of people regardless of their socioeconomic status.

VinFuture Prize CATEGORIES

One Vinfuture Grand Prize - US$ 3 Million & Three Additional Special Vinfuture Prizes - US$ 500,000 Each, Dedicated To:

  • Developing country innovators
  • Women innovators
  • Outstanding achievements in emerging fields
Learn more about Nomination Process

VinFuture Prize Council

The VinFuture Prize Council is a global and diverse team of distinguished individuals from academia, research and industry, all of whom are globally renowned for their achievements and contribution in the field of science, technology and industry in advancing human progress.
